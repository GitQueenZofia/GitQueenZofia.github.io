Game Mechanics

Dive deep into the world of RuneSkate

Core Mechanics

Fluid Movement

In RuneSkate, stopping means failing. The movement system is designed for constant flow. Chain your jumps, wall-rides, and slides.

Use the environment to your advantage. Every wall is a path, and every slope is an opportunity to gain massive speed.

Environment

Rails & Bounce Pads

The tunnel is your playground. Look for Twisted Rails to grind across gaps and recharge your stamina. Need to reach high places?

Jump onto the Trampolines scattered around the map. They will launch you into the air, allowing you to reach secret areas and hidden collectibles.

Economy

The Skate Shop

Collect floating Golden Coins during your run.

Visit the shop to spend your earnings.

The Finale

Boss Battle

At the end of the line waits Final Boss. This mechanical beast will test everything you've learned.
