About The Game

RuneSkate blends high-speed inline skating with fantasy adventure. Strap on your blades to grind rails and launch off trampolines in a world where gravity is just a suggestion.

Your mission is clear: collect mystical orbs to unlock new chambers, but don't stop moving. A relentless enemy is hunting you down. Can you outrun the stalker and survive the ultimate Boss Battle?
